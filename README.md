# Quang Anh Stocks

Quang Anh Stocks is a Vietnamese stock-market research product for exploring market activity, company fundamentals, and investment context in one place.

## What you can do

- Follow Vietnamese market indices, breadth, top movers, and sector heatmaps.
- View real-time stock prices, charts, order books, and price history.
- Explore company financial statements, financial ratios, holders, peers, and corporate events.
- Compare valuation indicators such as P/E and P/B across time and against industry peers.
- Screen the HOSE, HNX, and UPCOM universe by valuation, quality, growth, exchange, and industry.
- Monitor foreign trading flows, exchange rates, commodities, and macroeconomic indicators.
- Read market and company news alongside AI-assisted news summaries and live technical signals.
- Switch between Vietnamese and English across the product interface.

## Main areas

| Area | Highlights |
|---|---|
| Market overview | Indices, movers, heatmaps, market breadth, global markets |
| Stock detail | Price, Financials, Holders, Price History, News, Peers, Technical Analysis, Valuation |
| Stock screener | Filter stocks by ratios, profitability, growth, market cap, exchange, and ICB industry |
| Foreign trading | Net foreign buying and selling by value and volume |
| Macro | Vietnam and global indicators, FX, commodities, and GDP composition |
| Events & news | Corporate actions, dividends, shareholder events, and market news |

## Product principles

- Clear data presentation for everyday investors.
- Vietnamese-market context with bilingual interface support.
- News analysis is informational and is not investment advice.
- Market data can be delayed or incomplete; always verify before making investment decisions.

## Disclaimer

Quang Anh Stocks provides information for research and education only. Nothing on the website is investment advice, a recommendation, or an offer to buy or sell securities.
